Want to Know about Us?

In our world of today, fake news has only negative impacts on everything it comes across so I thought, why not create an easily accessible website that is loaded with the most informative content on how to discern, fight and mitigate the spread of fake news.

I embarked on the rewarding journey of gathering information and using my web development skills to create a medium whereby the information can be displayed and easily accessed by those willing to learn the ways of combatting the peril called Fake News.

Our Motto ✨Realism is a gem✨ encompasses everything this website stands for which is Possessing the knowledge and skills to detach your self from what is not genuine is a treasure.

Testimonials from people who have read the content this website offers are in the homepage and you can see that it testifies of how timeless & informative the articles are.

Our mission is to educate as much people, nations and organisations as we can and strenghten their minds and abilities in fighting fake news and ultimately eliminate the threat of fake news.
